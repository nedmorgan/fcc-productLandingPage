All-Inclusive Packages

We are able to provide all-inclusive services for all of our destination. This includes all air-fare, accomodations, food, drink, transportations, and many great activies at each of our exotic locations.

Amazing Destinations

Our destinations include some of the most awe-inspiring and picturesque locations in the world. Our flagship locations in Mayalsia, Mexico City, and Thailand allow our guests the freedom to be adventerous or enjoy a quiet holiday.

5-Star Cuisine

Each of our locations has a professionally trained culinary staff that specializes in cuisine local to your host country. All ingredients are locally-sourced and provided to the resorts daily.

Kinabatagan, Malaysia

Welcome to paradise hidden in the jungles of Borneo. This riverside location has an amazing array of wildlife, coupled with a beautiful retreat. During your 5-night stay you will be able to take many wildlife hikes and river boat rides to explore this magestic world. You can indulge in amazing local Malay cuisine. Our chef's have a collective 50 years of experience with foraging and preparing local cuisine. Every accomodation has a private bathroom and air-conditioning unit.

Price: $8,500

Koh Phi Phi, Thailand

This tiny island off the coast of Thailand offers some of the most incredible beaches and landscapes around. Our resort has a private beach with pool available for your comfort. Enjoy your 5-night all inclusive stay here with 5-star meals and amazing nightlife. We offer daily snorkel, SCUBA, and cave trips around the island.

Price: $9,500

Mexico City, Mexico

The capital of Mexico is not only one of the most populated cities in the world, but it is also an explosion of art and culture. You will be able to explore and experience this city with thousands of years of history. Our resort in Mexico City is nestled in the heart of the Condesa district. There are quiet streets and quaint sidewalk cafes. You will have the chance to get outside of the city during your 7-night stay to visit the Aztex ruins or take an exciting taco tour with our amazing local guides.

Price: $6,500
